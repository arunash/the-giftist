# The Giftist

**Business Deck — February 2026**

# Slide 1: The Problem

### Gifting is broken.

- **$400B** spent on gifts annually in the US alone — yet **65% of recipients** wish they'd gotten something different
- Gift registries are locked to single retailers (Amazon, Target) — missing most of what people actually want
- Group gifting is coordinated through messy group chats and Venmo requests
- No one knows what anyone actually wants — so we default to gift cards

**The result:** Billions wasted on unwanted gifts. Awkward conversations. Missed moments.

# Slide 2: The Solution

### Giftist — Your AI-powered gift concierge on WhatsApp

A universal wishlist + group gifting platform that lives where you already are: **WhatsApp, your browser, and the web.**

- Add anything from any store — products, experiences, trips, concert tickets, subscriptions
- AI concierge that learns your taste and recommends the perfect gift
- Friends and family chip in together with transparent funding progress
- No app to download. No retailer lock-in. Works everywhere.

# Slide 4: Key Feature — AI Gift Concierge

### A personal shopper that actually knows you

The Gift Concierge is an AI assistant trained on your taste profile, wishlist history, and upcoming events.

**What it does:**
- Recommends specific products (real brands, real prices) based on your preferences
- Learns your gifting circles — who you buy for, what they like, key dates
- Creates events and adds items to them conversationally
- Knows your budget, style, and past purchases
- Available on web chat and WhatsApp — same conversation, any device

**Free:** 10 messages/day | **Gold:** Unlimited

# Slide 5: Key Feature — Group Gift Funding

### Five friends. One gift. Zero guesswork.

Any item on a Giftist can be **crowdfunded** by friends and family:

- **Transparent progress** — real-time funding bar on every item
- **Any amount** — contribute $5 or $500, no minimums
- **Secure payments** — Stripe-powered checkout
- **Anonymous option** — contribute without revealing your identity
- **Giftee withdraws** — money goes to the recipient's bank via Stripe Connect

# Slide 14: Technology Stack

### Built for speed, privacy, and scale

- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, React, Tailwind CSS — fast, responsive, SEO-optimized
- **AI:** Claude (Anthropic) for concierge + recommendations, GPT for image analysis
- **Payments:** Stripe Checkout + Stripe Connect (payouts to giftees)
- **Messaging:** WhatsApp Business API via Meta Graph API, Twilio Verify for auth
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Neon) with Prisma ORM
- **Hosting:** Vercel (serverless, global edge)
- **Security:** HMAC-signed tokens, SSRF protection, CSP headers, Zod input validation
